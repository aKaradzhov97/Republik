Berliner 'Oktoberfest'

23.12.2018

Enjoy typical Bavarian Oktoberfest Party in Berlin from 19th of September – 04th of October. Try 18 different draft beer or our special Oktoberfest „Mass“ beer with traditional food. This liquid gold is probably the most important thing at Oktoberfest. Yes, it is Berlin which builds up the new “Oktober Fest”. Munich is out, Berlin is in! Therefore the Berliner Republic starts to celebrate on Saturday, 19.09.2015 with traditional Bavarian specialties, good German beer and pretty girls in dirndl dresses. So do not miss the chance to book one of our specially created festival boxes and let the party start.

Daily lunch

07.04.2018

It is about midday and your borborygmus develops an independent existence? The Berliner Republik is your knight in shining amour! From 12 am to 3 pm we serve our guests our daily changing lunch. For a cost of just 6,80 Euros you can enjoy a delicious 2-course menu with traditional „Berliner“ food and seasonal side dishes. Our service team is always in charge of presenting you today’s delicacies. And for the ones who loves to have lunch outside, the Berliner Republik offers a big and inviting terrace. Here you have a great overview of the river Spree and the Berlin city center!
